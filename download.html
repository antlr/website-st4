To use maven, refer to group ID org.antlr and artifact ID ST4 in your pom.xml file: 
<dependency> 
  <groupId>org.antlr</groupId> 
  <artifactId>ST4</artifactId> 
  <version>4.3.4</version> 
</dependency>
